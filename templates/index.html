About the Initiative

Transforming Police Accountability in Gujarat

The Gujarat Police Citizen Feedback System is a flagship digital initiative under the Smart Policing vision. It bridges the gap between citizens and law enforcement by providing a transparent, anonymous, and secure platform for feedback.

Secure & Anonymous

Personal details are optional. All data is encrypted end-to-end.

Mobile-First Design

Works on any device. Simply scan the QR code to get started.

AI-Powered Insights

Automatic sentiment analysis helps identify patterns quickly.

Simple Process

How It Works

Three simple steps to submit your feedback

01

Scan QR Code

Find the QR code displayed at your local police station and scan it with your smartphone camera.

02

Rate & Review

Fill in your experience ratings for staff behavior, response time, cleanliness, and overall service.

03

Get Confirmation

Receive your unique acknowledgment ID. Your feedback is processed and reviewed by officers.
